A Family Tradition of Excellence

Welcome to PRIME GRILL 1302, where family values meet culinary excellence in the heart of Condado. Our story is one of passion, dedication, and a commitment to bringing you the finest dining experience in San Juan.

We believe that great food brings people together. That's why we've created a welcoming, family-style atmosphere where every guest is treated like part of our extended family. From our prime hand-cut meats to our carefully curated selection of seafood, poultry, and innovative dishes, every plate tells a story of quality and care.

Located in the vibrant Condado neighborhood, we source the best local ingredients from across Puerto Rico, ensuring that each dish celebrates the rich flavors of our island while maintaining the highest standards of culinary excellence.
